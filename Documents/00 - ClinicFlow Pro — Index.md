## 🏥 Project Summary

ClinicFlow Pro is a **lightweight, cloud-based clinic management application** built for small and independent medical clinics across India. It eliminates manual, paper-based workflows by digitising patient management, prescriptions, appointments, follow-ups, and revenue tracking.

> [!info] Problem Statement
> Small Indian clinics operate largely on paper — causing time inefficiency, patient tracking gaps, prescription errors, failed follow-ups, and invisible revenue leakage.
