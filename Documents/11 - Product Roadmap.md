## 1. Roadmap Overview

The ClinicFlow Pro product roadmap outlines the planned feature delivery across two phases over 12 months. Phase 1 delivers the core MVP to address the most critical operational gaps. Phase 2 builds on Phase 1 with advanced features, offline support, and expanded integrations. All timelines assume a March 2024 project start.

## 2. Phase 1 — MVP (Months 1–7)

### Sprint Plan

| Sprint | Months | Features Delivered | Story Points | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sprint 1 | 1–2 | User Authentication (RBAC + OTP), Patient Registration, Patient Search, Visit History, Patient ID Generation | 26 | 📋 Planned |
| Sprint 2 | 2–3 | Digital Prescription Form, Drug Database Autocomplete, Prescription PDF Generation, Appointment Scheduler, Calendar View | 34 | 📋 Planned |
| Sprint 3 | 3–4 | Appointment SMS Reminders, Follow-Up Scheduling, Automated Follow-Up SMS, Follow-Up Dashboard, Audit Logging | 28 | 📋 Planned |
| Sprint 4 | 4–5 | Invoice Generation with GST, Payment Gateway Integration (Razorpay), Revenue Dashboard, Prescription History View | 30 | 📋 Planned |
| Sprint 5 | 5–6 | Pharmacy WhatsApp Dispatch, Revenue Report (PDF + Excel Export), Patient Activity Report, Admin User Management | 25 | 📋 Planned |
| Sprint 6 | 6–7 | Bug Fixing, UAT Support, Performance Optimization, Security Hardening, Training Material Preparation, Go-Live | Buffer | 📋 Planned |

## 3. Phase 2 — Enhanced Features (Months 8–12)

| # | Feature | Business Value | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| P2-01 | Offline Mode (PWA — no internet required for core features) | Enables use in low-connectivity rural / semi-urban areas | **Must Have** 🔴 |
| P2-02 | Multi-Doctor Clinic Support (multiple doctor profiles per clinic) | Enables group clinics and polyclinics to use the platform | **Must Have** 🔴 |
| P2-03 | Lab Test Ordering and Results Management | Integrates lab workflow into doctor consultation | **Should Have** 🟡 |
| P2-04 | Patient Mobile App (Android) for self-service | Patients can book, view prescriptions, and get reminders via app | **Should Have** 🟡 |
| P2-05 | WhatsApp Chatbot for patient appointment booking | Zero-app-download experience for patients | **Could Have** 🟢 |
| P2-06 | AI-Based Prescription Suggestions (based on diagnosis) | Reduces doctor cognitive load; prevents common drug interactions | **Could Have** 🟢 |
| P2-07 | Insurance Claims Integration (NHPM / Ayushman Bharat) | Supports patients covered under government health schemes | **Could Have** 🟢 |
| P2-08 | Advanced Analytics — Patient Insights Dashboard | Clinic owner gets demographic, disease, and revenue trends | **Could Have** 🟢 |
